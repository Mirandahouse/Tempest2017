About vivekananda Society

" Arise, Awake and stop not till goal is reached."
Vivekananda Society of Miranda House welcomes you all to its annual fest "YOUTHSAV"; May we learn and grow together. Here at Vivekananda Society we try to understand the philosophy and ideals of Swami Vivekananda and try to inculcate the feeling of brotherhood and peace in our daily lives. Vivekananda believed a lot in the power of youth. He always preached to live with a never dying spirit. After all, Youth is the time to do something and be someone!

Events in YOUTHSAV

1. Capture and Release ( Photography Competition)
2. Weave a story ( Story writing Competition)

Rules for Capture and release

1.The photograph can be clicked from any device(DSLR, Cell-phone, etc).
2.The theme(s) for the competition will be provided on the spot.
3.A maximum of 2 hours will be provided to click the photograph and submit it in both softcopyand hardcopy.
4.Participants are required to click the photograph in the assigned time period only.
5.No plagiarism will be entertained. Doing so will lead to disqualification.
6.A Caption is necessary with the photograph.

Rules for Weave a story

1.Participants are invited to release their creativity.
2.There should be no plagiarism.
3.Use of cell phones during the competition is not allowed.
4.The theme(s) will be provided on the spot.
5.Only Papers will be provided.
6.Word limit: 1500-2000 words.

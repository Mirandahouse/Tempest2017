About Debating Society

The Miranda House Debating Society is extremely proud to present the Annual Bilingual Shama Kohli Memorial Debate to be held on 26th February as a part of Miranda House's Annual Cultural Fest -Tempest 2017

Debate

Each college can send one team
The team cap for the competition is 15 teams
Rules for the debate are as follows -
1. Each speaker may choose to speak either in Hindi or English
2. Each speaker will get 3+ 1 =4 minutes of speaking
3. Speeches will be followed by one minute of interjection from the audience

The prize money up for grabs is as follows
Best team : 3000
Best speaker for and against : 2000 each

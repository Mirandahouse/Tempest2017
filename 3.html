About Anukriti

The Hindi Dramatics Society of Miranda House was conceptualized in 1957 by Anuradha kapoor and Hema Singh. It is one of the oldest theatre societies in Delhi University. They produce 3 productions every year, a street play, one act play and a full length production. Anukriti is known for it's grand Annual Full Length Productions which are usually directed by eminent theatre personalities.
